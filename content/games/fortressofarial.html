Fortress Of Arial


Screenshots:

Description:
Save the townspeople (@) from the arrows (_). There are regular arrows, fire arrows, and bomb arrows threatening the townspeople, and you must place letters to protect the townspeople from these threats.


Features:

  • Three types of materials for letters: hay, wood, and stone.
  • Three types of arrows: regular, fire, and bomb.
  • Strategy and action.

Known Bugs:

  • I've heard at least one bug report of an "Unexpected Error" in the Mac version, but I cannot reproduce it on my own machine. Please email me with a description of your computer and OS if you run into it.


Download:
Available for PC and Mac. If either version fails to run on your computer, please email me at "greg {at] czerniak [dot} info" (written that way to prevent spam) with a description of your computer and OS.
